⚠️ DISCLAIMER
Peace & Conflict is an informational platform only. All content — including conflict data, news headlines, AI analysis, and risk assessments — is provided for educational and awareness purposes. It does not represent the views of any government, military, or intelligence agency. AI-generated analysis is not a substitute for professional advice. The app makes no warranties regarding the accuracy or completeness of any information displayed.
1. DATA WE COLLECT
We collect no personal data on our servers. All user preferences (notification settings, account details) are stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to us.

• Account info: Username and email are stored locally on your device
• No tracking: No analytics, no cookies, no advertising IDs
• No location data: We never access your GPS or location
2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
This app connects to external services to deliver live content:

• RSS News Feeds: Al Jazeera, Anadolu Agency, UN News, DW, France 24, Sky News, RFI, NPR, The Diplomat, Global Voices, Euronews, CNA, Chatham House — subject to their own privacy policies

• AI Analysis: Powered by Anthropic Claude API. Conflict data sent to Anthropic for analysis is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

• rss2json.com: Used as a CORS proxy to fetch RSS feeds
٣. Notifications
Push notifications are processed entirely on your device. We use your browser's built-in Notification API. No notification data is sent to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
4. PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTIONS
Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information. All billing is managed by Google. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. Cancel anytime via Google Play > Subscriptions.
5. CONTENT ACCURACY
News content is sourced from third-party RSS feeds and displayed as-is. AI analysis is generated automatically and may contain errors. Conflict status indicators (Critical / High / Elevated) are algorithmic estimates and do not reflect official assessments by any government or organisation.
6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
This app contains news and information about armed conflicts and geopolitical events. It is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
7. CONTACT
For privacy questions or data requests, contact us via the Support button in the app. We respond within 48 hours.
8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
